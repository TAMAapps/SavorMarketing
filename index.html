My Story

I'm a food obsessed human who got tired of forgetting amazing meals. You know when a friend asks "what was that incredible ramen place in Tokyo?" and your mind goes blank? That drove me crazy.

After years of losing food photos in my camera roll and having debates about which city had better pizza with zero data to back it up, I said enough.

The breaking point came at a tiny trattoria in Rome. I couldn't compare their carbonara to one I'd had in New York years earlier. I realized I needed a better system.

So I built one. First just for me, a simple way to snap, rate, and actually remember every dish. When some of my friends asked to use it too, I realized I wasn't alone in this problem.

Now I'm sharing Savor with fellow food lovers. If enough people find it useful, I'll keep improving it and building the ultimate tool for those who take meals seriously.

Because if you can't tell someone exactly why last Tuesday's tacos were better than Austin's, are you even a real foodie?

My Mission

Help serious food people build their own taste database. No more random Yelp reviews. This is about your palate, your memories, your food story.
