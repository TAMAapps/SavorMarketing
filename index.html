👨‍🍳 Our Story

About Savor

Born from a simple frustration: forgetting incredible dishes and where we found them. As passionate food lovers and frequent travelers, we realized we were losing precious culinary memories faster than we could create them.

That late-night conversation in a small trattoria in Rome became the spark for Savor. We envisioned a world where every amazing meal could be captured, organized, and rediscovered years later with the same excitement as the first bite.

Today, Savor combines cutting-edge AI with intuitive design to help food enthusiasts around the world build their personal culinary legacy. Because every dish tells a story, and every story deserves to be remembered.

Our Mission

To transform how people connect with their culinary experiences, ensuring that no amazing dish is ever forgotten and every food memory becomes part of a lasting, searchable, and shareable journey.
