# Trump booed and cheered at the Kennedy Center while attending ‘Les Misérables’

**Date:** Yesterday

**Source:** [AP News](https://apnews.com/article/trump-kennedy-center-performing-arts-487a890419ebf4de42660c5d947dc69c)

**Summary:**
A tuxedo-wearing President Donald Trump was both booed and cheered as he took his seat for the opening night of "Les Misérables" at the Kennedy Center in Washington, DC, bringing his own dose of political drama to the theatrical production that was unfolding onstage. The event drew a mix of reactions from the audience, highlighting the charged political atmosphere surrounding the former president.
