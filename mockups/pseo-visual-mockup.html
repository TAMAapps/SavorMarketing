Perfect Pasta Carbonara: The Authentic Italian Recipe

By Chef Marco Rossi
• March 15, 2025 • 15 min read • Easy

Recipe Overview

15
Prep Time
10
Cook Time
4
Servings
★★☆
Difficulty

Carbonara is one of Rome's most beloved pasta dishes, and when made correctly, it's absolutely divine. This authentic recipe uses just five simple ingredients to create a creamy, rich sauce without any cream at all.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti or tonnarelli
  • 200g guanciale (or pancetta), diced
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

1

Prepare the pasta water

Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. The water should taste like the sea – this is crucial for properly seasoned pasta.

2

Cook the guanciale

In a large skillet, cook the diced guanciale over medium heat until crispy and golden, about 8-10 minutes. The fat should render out completely.

3

Prepare the egg mixture

In a bowl, whisk together egg yolks, grated Pecorino Romano, and a generous amount of black pepper until well combined.
