Key Design Principles

  • Minimal text per screen for mobile-first experience
  • Luxury aesthetic with premium gradients and gold accents
  • Behavioral psychology triggers (loss aversion, social proof)
  • Personal connection through founder story
  • Clear value proposition highlighting dish-specific rating USP
  • Emotional journey from problem identification to solution
  • Community-focused messaging for food critics and enthusiasts

Removed Screens

Screens 5 (Unique Value), 7 (Community), and 8 (Final CTA) have been removed from the final flow to streamline the user experience and reduce cognitive load. The remaining 6 screens provide a complete conversion journey without overwhelming users.
