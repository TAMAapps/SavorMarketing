Key Design Insights Applied

Personal Connection First

Starts with Alex's authentic story, making users feel they're supporting a real person who shares their passion for food.

Community Over Product

Emphasizes joining a foodie community and supporting fellow food lovers before demonstrating features.

Emotional First, Features Second

Like premium apps, we lead with emotional connection to food memories before showing scanning technology.

Progressive Disclosure

Seven screens build understanding gradually, preventing cognitive overload while maintaining deep engagement.

Personal Investment

Interactive questions and storytelling create sense of ownership before users even use the app.

Quantified Benefits

Specific metrics (3 seconds) make the value proposition concrete and memorable.
