## Visual Design Patterns Identified

### Hero Section Patterns
1. **Clear Value Propositions**: All sites lead with compelling headlines
2. **Visual Hierarchy**: Large headlines, supporting text, prominent CTAs
3. **Background Imagery**: Mix of gradients, photos, and illustrated backgrounds
4. **Call-to-Action Placement**: Consistently above-the-fold positioning

### Color Scheme Analysis
- **Tech Platforms**: Often use blue/purple gradients (Linear, Notion, Miro)
- **Wellness Apps**: Calming blues and greens (Calm)
- **Financial Services**: Professional blues and trustworthy tones (Stripe, Ellevest)
- **Food/Lifestyle**: Warm oranges and appetizing colors (BlueApron)
- **Creative Platforms**: Vibrant, energetic color palettes (Skillshare, Unbounce)

### Layout Structure Patterns
1. **Navigation**: Clean, minimal top navigation bars
2. **Hero Sections**: Large, centered content with strong visual focus
3. **Feature Grids**: 3-4 column layouts for feature showcases
4. **Social Proof**: Customer testimonials and statistics prominently displayed
5. **Progressive Disclosure**: Information revealed in digestible sections

### Typography Trends
- **Headlines**: Large, bold, often custom fonts
- **Body Text**: Clean, readable sans-serif fonts
- **Hierarchy**: Clear size and weight differentiation
- **Spacing**: Generous white space for readability

### Interactive Elements
- **Primary CTAs**: Bright, contrasting colors that stand out
- **Secondary Actions**: Subtle, outlined or text-based buttons
- **Hover States**: Subtle animations and state changes
- **Mobile Optimization**: Responsive design across all platforms

## Content Strategy Patterns

1. **Problem/Solution**: Identify pain point, present solution
2. **Feature/Benefit**: Highlight capabilities and user outcomes
3. **Social Proof**: Customer success stories and testimonials
4. **Urgency/Scarcity**: Time-limited offers or exclusive access

### Target Audience Positioning
- **Professional Tools** (Linear, Miro, Stripe): Efficiency and productivity focus
- **Consumer Apps** (Calm, Skillshare): Personal growth and development
